Love Poem

I keep a flower in my pocket,

And ribbons in hair.

A picture in my locket,

That I always like to wear.


I have got memories in a shoebox,

And sunshine on my mind.

I go for lots of walks,

And saved the leaves I find.


I keep rainbow in a flowerpot,

And letters under my bed.

I use bookmarks to save carefully,

The best things ever said.


I have got dreams in hidding places,

extra smiles for when I am blue.

But that place in my heart...

I am saving just for YOU!
